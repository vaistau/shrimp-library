Shrimp Library is a repository of random, highly specific knowledge gathered by a group of nifty ladies who decided to share it with the world. All the advice is tailored for women to meet their unique needs.

The name Shrimp Library comes as a result of internal jokes and memes. The point of this website is to try to ease women's lives.

We've all been through various bad experiences, and we survived, so we're here to help other women who might be going through the same in a way that will simplify the process. Learn from other people's mistakes.
